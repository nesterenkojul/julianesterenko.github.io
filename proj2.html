Russian Independent Media on YouTube. Surviving 2022

This dashboard provides a view of how Russian independent media and journalists got through 2022 - a year when war and oppression of the state forced its active opposition to leave the country. Even in exile, the most influential independent media channels on YouTube continued their work of fighting Russian propaganda and sharing a different view on current events - all shown by YT channel statistics.

Link to the dashboard on Tableau Public.

The Python notebook with data collection and exploration is in the GitHub repository.

One of the hardest parts of this project was getting access to YouTube channel IDs. This information is needed in order to get channel statistics via YouTube API. In this blog post, I share a hack on how to do that.

The dashboard displays the summarized statistics of all featured channels and can also be filtered by individual channels or their combinations.

The visualization is not perfect, but this topic is very important to me. With this work, I tried to show there are many people who do not support the current Russian regime and many media outlets that provide information to those people.

Vacation in Thailand. The Bigger Picture

This interactive data story lets you explore a trip to Thailand from a statistics standpoint. Some insights from 2010's data by Thailand's Ministry of Tourism and Sports.

Link to the dashboard on Tableau Public.

I came up with this topic simply because I am a regular traveler to Thailand. It is one of the best places on earth. I have been there 9 times and plan on many more. So I have decided to explore what it is like to be a tourist in Thailand in terms of statistics and maybe even find myself as a tiny part of this data.

The quest for needed data was quite challenging, but in the end, nicely detailed datasets were found at National Statistical Office of Thailand's website. The data was in an Excel spreadsheet format, so it needed some adjustments and cleaning before being used in Tableau.

I have chosen to make the dashboard long, scrollable, and landing-like in order to make interactive charts easier to use without moving between pages. Also learned the concept of 'Parameters' in Tableau to create interchangeable charts.



A couple of months later, analysis of this data and this dashboard transformed into a graduation project (only available in Russian for now) for additional degree program Technologies of Data Analysis at ITMO University.

Immigrants of Modern Art

Immigrants have always been a driving force in all areas of human activity: from science and industry to art and culture. It particularly applies to the world of modern art with its broad variety of means, subjects and forms.

MoMA's artist dataset contains 15,544 records, each representing an artist who has works in MoMA's collection. 21% of them can be considered immigrants as their nationality differs from their place of birth. The largest immigrant group (417 artists) is of American nationality. They came from all over the globe - this work displays just a dozen of countries.

The visualization combines a treemap with an art "exhibition". Artworks have been chosen so that their authors are representatives of corresponding immigrant groups.

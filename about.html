About

Hi! I'm Julia Nesterenko and I visualize data.

My work is focused on creating data visualizations on a broad range of topics. I mainly use Tableau as a viz tool and Python for scraping, parsing, cleaning, and transforming data. For more artistic endeavours I prefer utilizing Flourish, Canva and Figma.

On a more serious note, I also specialize in data analysis: collecting data, transforming it, looking for patterns, testing hypotheses, answering business questions.

Currently enrolled in Intelligent Systems in Humanities (aka Digital Humanities) bachelor's program at ITMO University.
Working as a Laboratory Assistant in ITMO's Laboratory of Intelligent Systems and Applications.

Also completed some courses:
- additional degree program Technologies of Data Analysis;
- Fundamentals of Multimedia Data Processing, Data Visualization Methods, Data Storage and Processing by ITMO;
- Generation Python, Image Processing, Data Analysis in R by Stepik;
- Novaya Gazeta Europe's Investigative and Data Journalism Summer School.

I adore cooking and recipe development so much so I'm not afraid to post it on my nano-foodblog. Moreover, I regularly organize a dinner club.
From time to time I attend a movie club where we watch and discuss movies with my fellow cinemaholics.
I also used to be a professional swimmer for 10 years, so don't be afraid of my wardrobe-like shoulders and don't hesitate to invite me to a swimming pool :)
